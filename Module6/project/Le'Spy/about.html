About US

Our Business

At Le'Spy we are dedicated to providing you with the best coffee experience. We are committed to fair trade practices. We believe in rewarding our farming partners for their hard work, hence we purchase our beans directly from farmers eliminating the middle man.
We also believe in supporting other local business in Minneapolis, so all our non coffee products and ingredients are sourced locally.

Our Process

Our committment to quality is uncompromised. We grind and roast our beans each morning. All coffee orders are hand made, and crafted to perfection. As a matter of principle, we do NOT use automatic coffee machine.
Our pastires are parisian inspired. All pastries are fresh made each day. We serve artsian cakes, seasonal tarts, ccokies and croissants. All our sandwiches are made in house at the time of order.

Our Goals

At Le'Spy, coffee is about a craft, it is about bringing people together. So if you need a place to study or get some work done, a meet up spot for that first(or second) date, a cool place to vibe with friends, or just a spot to hang out by yourself, we are the right place for you.
