Welcome to Le'Spy

We are a locally owned and independent cafe in uptown Minneapolis. At Le'Spy we aim to provide you with the best coffee experience ever. We roast our coffee beans onsite daily. All our pastry items are freshly baked each morning.
We are committed to perfecting coffee excellence. We cater to clients with varying dietary needs. We ensure that every cup of java, every bag of joe, and every morsel of our pastries are made with love and with the freshest of ingredients.
